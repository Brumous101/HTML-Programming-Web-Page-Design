Why I love Halloween

Halloween is the best holiday because the environment surrounding the holiday is counterculture.

It's the only day of the year where people are able to be someone they aren't the other 364.25 days a year.

The weather is changing, the leaves are changing, and it gives the idea that your personal identity can change too.

Go get some free candy, hit the gym, and ask your crush out on a date! If you don't have a date there is no shame in catching a scary movie by yourself (see showings above).

"Man is least himself when he talks in his own person. Give him a mask, and he will tell you the truth." ~Oscar Wilde

It's because of all of these reasons that Halloween > Christmas.
