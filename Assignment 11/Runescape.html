Oldschool Runescape

Runescape has always been a constant in my life. Between girlfriends, between friendships, and between classes. Runescape has always been there! I am a veteran of the game at this point. I've played for the past 13 years or so and could tell you anything and everything about the game. I've created probably 5 different accounts and actively play on 2 of them currently. I started with a normal account that could use all the functions in the game and then in 2014 Jagex (the owner of the game) made a game mode that makes it to where you can not trade with other players making you not in the economy and being your own.

This was a good thing as previous to that the best way to get ahead in the game was to play 4 accounts or more at the same time. I started the gamemode and eventually got bored and tried the extra hard game mode where you can only carry 28 items and can't use the storage facilities in game.
